The problem

Your people are tired in a way a long weekend can't fix.

Real time in nature does what a webinar cannot. It lowers stress, clears the head and rebuilds trust between people.

Burnout is not a bad mood, it is a running cost. It shows up as sick days, quiet quitting, good people handing in notice and a team that used to have spark going flat. Most corporate wellness stops at fruit and a webinar. It never touches the thing that is actually worn down.

Iylia, your guide

Meet your guide

He used to build bodies. Now he rebuilds heads.

Iylia ran a gym for years. He watched people chase a stronger body while their sleep, their focus and their calm quietly fell apart. Strong on the outside, running on empty underneath. He figured out the work he cared about was never really about muscle.

So he swapped the gym floor for forest trails, waterfalls and quiet coast. Someone who understands training, recovery and how the body holds stress, walking your team back to steady, one route at a time.

  • Ex gym owner and coach
  • Breathwork and movement
  • Guides SG and Johor

Where we go

Wild places, closer than you think.

Singapore

A city in nature

Rare wild hides in plain sight here. The treetop walk high in the MacRitchie canopy, the old primary rainforest of Bukit Timah, the southern ridgelines, and the quiet coastal trails of Sentosa. We move through them softly and leave them better.
Johor

Ancient rainforest

Across the causeway the country turns wild. The old growth forest and waterfalls of Endau Rompin, rivers to camp beside, and land the Orang Asli have called home for generations. This is where the Immersion goes.

How we tread

We borrow these places. We give them back better.

Leave no traceWe pack out what we bring, stay on the trails, and keep the wild wild.
Learn from the landWe walk with local and Orang Asli guides, and pay into their communities.
Give backA part of every retreat goes to keeping these places protected.
