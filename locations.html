Where we go

Wild places, closer than you think.

Rare, protected wild sits right on the city's doorstep. We move through it softly, and cross into the deeper forest of Johor for the longer trips.

Singapore

A city in nature

Rainforest, reservoirs and coast, all within reach of the office.

Johor

Ancient rainforest

Old growth forest, waterfalls and Orang Asli land across the causeway.

Singapore

A city that protects its wild.

Reservoir

MacRitchie Reservoir

Old rainforest wrapped around the water, shaded boardwalks, and the TreeTop Walk, a suspension bridge high in the canopy. Our favourite half-day reset.

Rainforest

Bukit Timah Nature Reserve

One of the last patches of primary rainforest anywhere, and the island's highest natural point. Quiet, dense, and full of life.

Ridgeline

The Southern Ridges

Forest ridgelines linked by the Henderson Waves bridge, where the treetops meet the city skyline.

Coast

Sentosa's quiet trails

Away from the crowds, shaded rainforest paths and calm beaches for a gentle, easy day out.

Johor

Where the country turns wild.

Ancient forest

Endau Rompin

Ancient rainforest on the Johor border, huge waterfalls, and Orang Asli guides who have known this land for generations. Where the Immersion goes.

Waterfalls

Kota Tinggi

Falls to swim under and riverside camps beneath the canopy, an easy crossing from Singapore.

Highlands

Rivers and hill country

Cooler air, wilder ground, and real distance from the desk for the multi-day trips.
