Programs

Three ways in.

Every retreat is shaped around your team, your goals and your group size. Here is where most companies start. Pricing is built to fit, so just ask.

R1

The Reset

Half day / Singapore

A focused morning of movement, breathwork and a nature walk. The simplest way to give a stretched team a real pause without losing a whole day.

  • 3 to 4 hours
  • Up to 20 people
  • Within Singapore
  • All fitness levels
  • Guided by Iylia

A typical morning

  • Arrive and unplugPhones away. We open with slow movement and breathwork to shake off the week.
  • Move through natureA gentle guided walk through rainforest or reservoir, paced so everyone stays together.
  • Reset and closeStillness, a few honest prompts, and one or two habits to carry back to the desk.
R2

The Day Out

Full day / Singapore or Johor

A complete day in nature. Movement, a guided trail or waterfall, a shared meal and real reflection. The signature Flow with Chills experience.

  • Full day
  • Up to 30 people
  • Singapore or Johor
  • Shared meal included
  • All fitness levels

How the day runs

  • Arrive and unplugWe land everyone in the same place with breathwork and slow movement.
  • Move through itA guided trail, a waterfall or a stretch of coast. Built to get people moving together, not competing.
  • Rest and resetStillness, a shared meal, and simple prompts that reconnect the team as people.
  • Carry it backEveryone leaves with a small, honest set of habits to protect their energy.
R3

The Immersion

Multi day / Johor

Our deepest reset. A few days off the grid in the Johor wild, living close to nature and learning from the people who know the forest best.

  • 2 to 3 days
  • Tailored group size
  • Johor retreat sites
  • Camping and meals
  • Orang Asli guides

A sample trip

  • Day oneRide in, set up camp by the river, an easy trail and a first night under the canopy.
  • Day twoDeeper into the forest with Orang Asli guides. Honey, bushcraft, waterfalls and wild swims.
  • Heading homeA slow morning, a final sit by the water, and the drive back properly rested.

Inside the Immersion

Five things your team will remember.

The moments that make the multi-day trip worth it.

  • 01

    Ride through the forest

    Guided horseback trails through the trees. No experience needed.

  • 02

    Honey with the Orang Asli

    Harvest hive honey and learn forest craft from indigenous guides.

  • 03

    Camp under the canopy

    Nights by the river, around a fire, far from any screen.

  • 04

    Waterfalls and wild swims

    Trek to hidden falls and cool off in clear jungle water.

  • 05

    Fire and forest skills

    Fire without matches, edible plants, and reading the land.
