About

The man walking your team in.

Iylia has spent his whole working life helping people feel stronger. These days he does it in the forest, not the gym.

From the gym floor to the forest.

Iylia ran a gym for years. He coached people through early mornings and hard sets, and he loved it. But he kept noticing the same thing. People were building stronger bodies while their sleep, their focus and their calm quietly fell apart. Strong on the outside, running on empty underneath.

He realised the work he actually cared about was never really about muscle. It was about helping people feel like themselves again. And the place that did that best was never the gym. It was outside.

Strong on the outside, running on empty underneath.

Why nature.

So he traded the gym floor for forest trails, waterfalls and quiet coast. He learned the routes, the rhythms and the small rituals that help a tired mind switch off. Breathwork, slow movement, real stillness, and time in places where the noise of work simply cannot reach.

He understands training and recovery, and he understands how the body carries stress. That is what he brings to every retreat. Someone who can read a group, set the right pace, and walk your team back to steady.

Respect for the wild.

Iylia is careful about the places he brings people to. We tread lightly, we leave no trace, and we learn from the people who know the land best, including the Orang Asli guides who host our deeper trips in Johor. A part of every retreat goes back into keeping these places protected.

What he brings.

  • Former gym owner and coach
  • Breathwork and movement
  • Trail and nature guiding
  • Guides across Singapore and Johor
